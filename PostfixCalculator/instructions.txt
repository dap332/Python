To use enter problem in postfix notation.
'+' for addition
'-' for subtraction
'*' for multiplication
'/' for division
'**' for power

Works for nonnegative numbers only
No parentheses

Handles errors and bad inputs pretty well.


separate each digit and symbol with a space
e.g

3 + 4 * 2 would be entered as
3 4 2 + *
and would output 11

Takes input from stdin.
pressing enter will go to a new line and allow another input. EOT ends the program.
